FAQ
  • How do I create an account?

    To create an account, click on the "Account" button on the homepage. Fill in the required information and follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

  • How can I search for jobs?

    To search for jobs, go to the search bar on the homepage or the "all jobs" button. Enter relevant keywords, location, and any other filters you prefer. Click on the "Search job" button to see the job listings that match your criteria.

  • How do I apply for a job?

    To apply for a job, click on the "view details" button of the job listing you're interested in. Read the job description and requirements carefully. If you meet the qualifications, click on the "Apply Now" button and follow the instructions to submit your application.

  • Can I save job listings for later?

    Yes, you can save job listings for later. When viewing a job listing, look for the "Save" button. Click on it, and the job will be added to your saved jobs section, allowing you to easily access it in the future.

  • How long does it take to hear back from employers after applying?

    The time it takes to hear back from employers can vary. Some employers may respond within a few days, while others may take weeks or even months. It's best to be patient and continue applying to other opportunities while you wait for a response.
